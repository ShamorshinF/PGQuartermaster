▸ How to use Quests (click to expand)
Views
🟡 My Active Quests
Shows only delivery quests currently in your quest log.
✅ Items Available
Shows quests where you already have the required item in storage — ready to turn in.
📋 Deliver Quests
Browse all known delivery quests in the game, whether active or not.
Features
🗺 Plan Route Generates an optimized route for completing your active delivery quests, grouping stops by zone. 📌 Active Pinned Pins your active quests to the top of the list so they're always visible. 👁 Show Hidden Reveals quests you've previously hidden. Use the checkbox column to hide completed quests. Search Filter by quest name, item name, NPC, or zone.
Table Tips
Column headers Click to sort by that column. Hide checkbox Check the box on the far right to hide a quest you've already completed. Only available when viewing "Items Available" or "Deliver Quests" — can't hide while filtering by active quests.
ℹ️ It is currently impossible to pull a list of all completed quests from imported character data, therefore the quest list may show quests you have already completed. Quests can be hidden from the list by checking the box in the column on the far right.
⚠️ Quests tagged as "Collect" have been excluded from this list, as they typically require you to harvest or gather items in the world rather than purchase them. However, the game's quest tagging is sometimes inconsistent — some purchasable delivery quests may have been excluded, and some harvest quests may appear in the list.

▸ How to use Work Orders (click to expand)
Main Actions
🚚 Plan Route
The main feature. Generates an optimized multi-stop route telling you where to go, what to pick up, what to craft, and where to turn in. Start here.
🛒 Shopping List
Shows every item you need across all completable work orders, with storage shortfalls and sub-ingredient breakdowns.
🔍 Filtered List
Same as Shopping List but scoped to only the work orders currently visible in the table after your filters.
🔄 Update Character
Re-imports your latest inventory and character data from the game's Reports folder without leaving this tab.
Filters
📜 Have Contract Only show orders you hold the contract for or have as an active quest. 🎯 Within My Level Hide orders above your character's current Industry level. 📍 Item in Zone Show only orders where the required item is stored in the same zone as the NPC. 🛒 Requires NPC Purchases Include orders where items must be bought from NPC vendors. Ind. Lvl slider Narrow the displayed orders to a specific Industry level range.
Craft Status Buttons
These color-coded buttons filter work orders by whether you can craft the required item:
✓ Ready You have the skill, recipe, and all ingredients in storage. Good to go. ◑ NPC Buy You have the skill and recipe, but some ingredients need to be purchased from an NPC vendor. ⚗ Partial You know the recipe and have the skill, but are missing some ingredients (not vendor-purchasable). 📖 No Recipe A recipe exists for this item, but you haven't learned it yet. ⬆ Low Skill Your skill level is too low to craft this item. 🚫 No Craft Hides all craftable items, showing only work orders that require non-craftable items.
Table Tips
Column headers Click to sort by that column. Item names Click to open the item's Project Gorgon wiki page. 🔨 button View the full crafting breakdown and ingredient list for that item. NPC / Zone Click to open the NPC or zone wiki page. Hover to see the other value.

Welcome to PG Quartermaster

A companion tool for Project Gorgon that helps you work smarter, not harder.

🚚 Route Planner — optimal routes for work order turn-ins
🔨 Craft Planner — what to craft, what to pick up, step by step
📚 Missing Recipes — find what you haven't learned yet
🗺 Quest Tracker — delivery quests and where to turn them in

📥 Getting Started

First, export your data from Project Gorgon:
  1. Open Settings in-game (gear icon)
  2. Go to the VIP tab
  3. Click Export Storage — saves your inventory as JSON
  4. Click Export Character — saves your character sheet as JSON
  5. Come back here and click One Click Initialization below
📁 Files are saved to: C:\Users\[you]\AppData\LocalLow\Elder Game\Project Gorgon\Reports
✨ One Click Initialization ✨
  • 📂 Opens your game's Reports folder and finds the most recent inventory and character JSON files
  • 📋 Loads your inventory — all items across every storage location
  • 📜 Loads your character sheet — skills, work orders, and progression data
  • 🌐 Fetches quest, item, and recipe data from the Project Gorgon CDN
